About me

A trusted presence for children and families

I'm Elizabeth, Co‑President of a tutoring & mentoring group I started in middle school — every student in our program earned A or B grades, and one advanced two grade levels in math within two months. My tutoring background reaches back to grades 7–8, giving me more than a decade of experience supporting learners.

I've worked at Bethesda Country Day School, captained varsity water polo (leadership & teamwork), participated in GirlsWhoCode (tech-forward tutoring), and volunteered with Ronald McDonald House. I hold First Aid/CPR certification and several MSDE trainings focused on supporting students with disabilities and classroom safety.

My approach blends structure with warmth — I prioritize social-emotional development alongside academic progress, using evidence-based practices from specialized trainings such as the University of Maryland Pyramid Model for preschool classrooms.
